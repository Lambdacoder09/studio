# **App Name**: BizManager

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure email and password-based authentication to restrict the single business owner's access to the dashboard and application features.
- Dashboard: Display an overview of the business including total sales, total purchases, total expenses, current stock value, today's sales, low stock alerts, and a list of recent transactions (sales and expenses).
- Inventory Management: Add, edit, and delete products. Track product name, SKU, category, purchase price, selling price, and current quantity. Provide a low-stock warning indicator.
- Sales Management: Create new sale entries by selecting products from inventory, entering quantity and price, and auto-calculating the total. Automatically reduce inventory quantity on each sale. Keep a history of sales.
- Purchase Recording: Record purchases from suppliers, including supplier name, product, quantity, purchase price, and date. Automatically increase stock levels.
- Expense Tracking: Add daily expenses with expense name, category, amount, and date fields. Allow users to view an expense list and a monthly expense summary.
- Financial Reporting: Generate basic financial reports, including a profit and loss summary, a comparison of total income versus expenses, and a stock valuation report, all filterable by date range.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#3F51B5) for a professional, trustworthy feel.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) to ensure readability and reduce eye strain.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) to highlight interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif with a modern, machined, objective, neutral look, suitable for headlines or body text
- Use clear, modern icons to represent each function. All icons will be simple and monochrome to keep the design clean and professional.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout to ensure that the application is easy to navigate and the key information is easy to find. The layout will be responsive and adaptable to different screen sizes.
- Use subtle transitions to provide feedback when an action is triggered. Avoid animations that are distracting or unnecessary.
